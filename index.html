Moon M. Rajib

Founder & CEO

Mr. Moon M. Rajib is the visionary Founder and CEO of SMART-trend Digital, a leading firm in digital marketing and web services provider in Bangladesh. With over eight years of experience in the ICT sector, he has made significant strides in branding, communication and digital marketing, previously holding roles at Bangladesh Association of Software and Information Services (BASIS), MetroNet Bangladesh Limited, AyAl Corp Limited and the Daily Kholakagoj- a daily newspaper. Mr. Rajib is also the founder of the Bangladesh ICT & Innovation Network (BIIN), showcasing his dedication to nurturing young talent in ICT. He holds a Bachelor’s in Computer Science and Engineering from Jagannath University and a Master’s in Media & Communications from IUB, with further qualifications from prestigious institutions like IBA, University of Dhaka. A prominent figure in the ICT community, Mr. Rajib’s career reflects a commitment to innovation, education, and leadership in digital technology.
